Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Visit our Courses page, select your desired course, and click the "Enroll Now" button. Fill out the enrollment form, and our team will contact you within 24 hours.

What are the payment options?

We accept various payment methods including M-Pesa, bank transfer, and credit/debit cards. Payment plans are also available for select courses.

Are the courses online or in-person?

We offer both online and in-person learning options. You can choose the format that best suits your schedule and learning style.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, upon successful completion of your course, you will receive an industry-recognized certificate from DAN TECH.
